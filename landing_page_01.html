About Me

Hello! I'm Mac (short for Mackenzie, if you were wondering). I thrive on the intersection of vintage aesthetics and modern development practices. My philosophy is simple: **user experience is paramount**, and clean code is the foundation for maintainable, scalable products.

I started my journey building simple static sites before diving into frameworks like React and Angular, specializing in creating robust frontends backed by efficient serverless or microservice architectures.

When I'm not coding, you can find me collecting vintage computer hardware or exploring national parks. I'm always looking for interesting new challenges and collaborations!
